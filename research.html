ANXIETY

An anxiety disorder is a common mental illness defined by feelings of uneasiness worry and fear. It is often sudden and unexpected and can happen when the person is happy and healthy, or asleep. It also usually happens away from home.


OBSESSIVE-COMPULSIVE DISORDER (OCD)

A common disorder in which one has uncontrollable, recurring thoughts and behaviors that they feel the urge to repeat over and over. These symptoms can interfere with all aspects of life, including school, work, and personal relationships.


BIPOLAR DISORDER

A common disorder with biological roots in the brain. Bipolar disorder can cause extreme mood swings. Manic(feeling very happy) and depressive(feeling very sad), episodes are the most commonly faced.


SCHIZOPHRENIA

A long-term mental disorder of a type involving a breakdown in the relation between thought, emotion, and behavior, leading to faulty perception, inappropriate actions and feelings, withdrawal from reality and personal relationships into fantasy and delusion, and a sense of mental fragmentation.


DEPRESSION

Depression is one of the most common mental disorder/illnesses in the average American population. Symptoms cause changes in how an individual feels, thinks, acts, goes through day to day activities, interacts, sleeps and eats.


EATING DISORDERS

Eating Disorders are serious conditions related to persistent eating behaviors that negatively impact your health, your emotions and your ability to function in important areas of life. The most common eating disorders are anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa and binge-eating disorder.
